As a client of CALZADO J&R, you can register yourself, browse the catalog, and place orders once your account is validated. This guide walks you through the complete process.

## Registration process

<Info>
Clients can register freely through the public registration form. You don't need an invitation or administrator approval to create an account.
</Info>

### Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to registration page">
    Go to the CALZADO J&R application at `http://localhost:5173` (or your company's deployment URL) and click on "Register" or "Sign Up".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in the registration form">
    Complete all required fields:
    - **First name** (Nombres): Your first name
    - **Last name** (Apellidos): Your last name
    - **Phone**: Your contact phone number
    - **Email**: Your email address (will be your username)
    - **Password**: Choose a secure password
    - **Business name** (optional): If you're ordering for a business, enter its name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit your registration">
    Click the submit button. The system creates your account with:
    - `is_validated=False` (pending validation)
    - `is_active=False` (inactive until validated)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation message">
    You'll see: "Account created successfully. Pending validation by administrator."
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Your account is created immediately, but you cannot log in until an administrator validates it.
</Note>

## Waiting for validation

After registering, your account enters a pending state:

1. An administrator receives notification of your registration
2. They review your account information
3. They validate your account when approved
4. Your account becomes active: `is_validated=True` and `is_active=True`

<Accordion title="How long does validation take?">
  Validation time depends on administrator availability. Typically:
  - During business hours: Within a few hours
  - Outside business hours: By next business day
  
  If you haven't heard back within 24 hours, contact CALZADO J&R directly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why is validation required?">
  Validation ensures:
  - Account authenticity
  - Business relationship verification
  - Accurate contact information
  - Prevention of spam or fraudulent accounts
</Accordion>

## First login

Once your account is validated, you can log in:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to login page">
    Navigate to the CALZADO J&R login page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    - **Email**: The email address you registered with
    - **Password**: The password you created during registration
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic redirect">
    After successful login, the system detects your role as a client and redirects you to your client dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Unlike employees, you do not need to change your password on first login since you created it yourself during registration.
</Info>

## Using your dashboard

Your client dashboard provides access to:

### Product catalog

- Browse available footwear products
- View detailed product information
- See pricing and availability
- Filter by style, size, or category

### Placing orders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the catalog">
    Navigate through available products to find what you need.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select products">
    Choose items and specify:
    - Quantity
    - Sizes needed
    - Any customization options
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review your order">
    Check your order summary:
    - Items and quantities
    - Total price
    - Delivery information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit the order">
    Confirm and place your order. You'll receive an order confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Order tracking

From your dashboard, you can:

- **View order history**: See all your past and current orders
- **Check order status**: Track where your order is in production
- **See order details**: Review items, quantities, and pricing
- **Download invoices**: Access order documentation

</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access account settings">
    Navigate to your profile or settings section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select change password">
    Click on "Change Password" option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter passwords">
    - Current password
    - New password
    - Confirm new password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Submit to update your password securely.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Session security

Your account uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for secure authentication:

| Token Type | Duration | Purpose |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Access token | 15 minutes | Active session authorization |
| Refresh token | 7 days | Automatic re-authentication |

- If you're inactive for 15 minutes, the system refreshes your session automatically
- After 7 days, you need to log in again
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)

## Your account information

The system stores these details about your account:

| Field | Description | Can Edit |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| First name | Your first name | Yes |
| Last name | Your last name | Yes |
| Email | Login username | No (contact admin) |
| Phone | Contact number | Yes |
| Business name | Optional business identifier | Yes |
| Account status | Validated and active | No |
| Registration date | When you created your account | No |
| Validated by | Which admin validated you | No |
| Validation date | When you were validated | No |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I can't log in after registering">
  Your account may still be pending validation. Check for:
  - A confirmation email about validation
  - Error message saying "Account pending validation"
  
  If you registered recently, wait for administrator approval. If it's been more than 24 hours, contact CALZADO J&R.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I forgot my password">
  Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page:
  1. Enter your email address
  2. Check your email for a password reset link
  3. Follow the link and set a new password
  4. Log in with your new password
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I need to change my business name">
  You can update your business name from your account settings:
  1. Navigate to your profile
  2. Edit the business name field
  3. Save changes
  
  The business name field is optional and can be left blank if you're ordering as an individual.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My order isn't showing up">
  If a placed order doesn't appear in your dashboard:
  - Refresh the page
  - Check your order history tab
  - Verify the order was successfully submitted (you should have received a confirmation)
  - Contact support if the order is missing after 30 minutes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel or modify an order?">
  Order modification depends on its status:
  - **Pending/Not started**: Contact an administrator to modify or cancel
  - **In production**: Modifications may not be possible
  - **Completed**: Cannot be changed
  
  Contact CALZADO J&R as soon as possible if you need to change an order.
</Accordion>

## Getting help

If you encounter issues not covered in this guide:

1. Check your account status (validated and active)
2. Verify your internet connection
3. Try logging out and back in
4. Clear your browser cache
5. Contact CALZADO J&R support with:
   - Your account email
   - Description of the issue
   - Any error messages you see
