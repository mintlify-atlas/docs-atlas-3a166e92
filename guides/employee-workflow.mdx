Employees work on assigned tasks based on their occupation. This guide covers how to get started and use your employee dashboard.

## Getting your account

<Note>
Employees cannot register themselves. Your account must be created by an administrator.
</Note>

When an administrator creates your employee account:

1. The system generates a secure temporary password
2. You receive an email with your login credentials:
   - Your email address (username)
   - Temporary password
3. Your account is created with these details:
   - First name (Nombres)
   - Last name (Apellidos)
   - Phone number
   - Email
   - Occupation (your role in production)

<Info>
Check your spam folder if you don't see the credentials email. Contact your administrator if you need the credentials resent.
</Info>

## Employee occupations

Your account is assigned one of these occupations:

- **Guarnición**: Upper assembly work
- **Solador**: Sole attachment
- **Cortador**: Cutting leather and materials
- **Emplantillador**: Insole preparation

Your occupation determines which tasks appear in your dashboard.

## First login

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to login page">
    Go to `http://localhost:5173` (or your company's deployment URL) and click on the login option.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter credentials from email">
    Enter the email and temporary password that were sent to you by the administrator.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="System detects your role">
    The system automatically identifies you as an employee and prepares to redirect you to the task dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Forced password change">
    Before accessing your dashboard, you **must** change your password. The system will display a password change form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You cannot skip the password change step. This security measure ensures only you know your password.
</Note>

## Changing your password

### On first login

When you log in for the first time, you'll see a password change form:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">
    This is the temporary password from the email.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    Choose a strong password that you'll remember.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm your new password">
    Re-enter the new password to confirm.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    After submitting, the system updates your password and sets `must_change_password=False`, allowing you to access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Changing password later

You can change your password anytime from your account settings:

1. Navigate to your profile or settings section
2. Look for "Change Password" option
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Submit to update

<Info>
Passwords are hashed using bcrypt for security. The system never stores your actual password.
</Info>

## Using your dashboard

After changing your password, you'll be redirected to your employee dashboard.

### Task dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- **Assigned tasks**: Work items assigned to you based on your occupation
- **Task status**: Whether tasks are pending, in progress, or completed
- **Task details**: Information about each assignment
- **Priority**: Urgent or standard tasks

### Viewing tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your task list">
    Your dashboard displays all tasks assigned to your occupation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a task">
    Click on a task to view full details including:
    - Order information
    - Required materials
    - Deadline
    - Special instructions
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update task status">
    Mark tasks as in progress or completed as you work through them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Session management

The system uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for authentication:

- **Access token**: Valid for 15 minutes
- **Refresh token**: Valid for 7 days

If you're inactive, your session will expire for security:
- After 15 minutes, you'll need to refresh
- After 7 days, you'll need to log in again

<Note>
If you see "Session expired," simply log in again with your credentials.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="I forgot my password">
  Contact your administrator to reset your password. They can generate a new temporary password and send it to your email. You'll need to change it on next login.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I don't see any tasks">
  This could mean:
  - No tasks have been assigned to your occupation yet
  - There's a filter active on your dashboard
  - Contact your administrator if you believe tasks should be visible
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I can't log in with my credentials">
  Verify that:
  - You're using the correct email address
  - Your caps lock is off when typing the password
  - You've changed your password if this is after your first login
  
  If problems persist, ask your administrator to check your account status.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My session keeps expiring">
  The access token expires after 15 minutes of use. The system should automatically refresh it. If you're experiencing issues:
  - Try logging out and back in
  - Clear your browser cache
  - Contact support if the problem continues
</Accordion>
